Booking Policies

What to know before you book.

Deposits are non-refundable

All deposits are non-refundable and applied to your total at the appointment. Cancellations forfeit the deposit. Transfers are allowed one time with sufficient advance notice.

Designs reviewed at appointment

Custom designs and stencils are created and reviewed during your appointment - not sent in advance. Bring reference images, sizing ideas, and placement preferences.

Rescheduling requires notice

Please provide as much notice as possible to reschedule. Same-day cancellations are subject to deposit forfeiture. Your deposit can transfer once with adequate advance notice.

Pricing confirmed at appointment

Final pricing is confirmed after the artist reviews your design, size, and placement. Starting prices are minimums and may increase based on detail and complexity.
