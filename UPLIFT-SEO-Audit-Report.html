Yelp Gap — What This Costs You

Nine Moons Piercing has 487 Yelp reviews. You have 135. On Yelp's "Top 10 Piercing near SoHo" list, that gap directly affects your rank position. In NYC, Yelp still drives 15–25% of local service discovery for walk-in businesses — it's where tourists and new residents search first. Adding Yelp review prompts to your post-appointment follow-up flow (text or email) could realistically add 10–15 reviews per month, closing the gap within 18–24 months. That alone could move you up multiple positions on the Yelp local list.
