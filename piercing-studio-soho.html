Curated Ear Piercing in SoHo

Curated ear piercing is the art of planning your ear as a full composition - selecting placement, jewelry, and layering intentionally. No random additions. A complete look, built over time.

Studio Policies

Jewelry Policy

All new piercings require jewelry purchased through UPLIFT. This ensures the correct material, gauge, and length for safe healing. Outside jewelry is not accepted for new piercings.

Age Policy

Clients must be 18 or older for most piercings. Minors may be accommodated for lobe piercings with a parent or legal guardian present and valid ID for both parties.

Check-Ups

Check-ups are complimentary at UPLIFT. Return any time to have your piercing checked. Downsizing and installs may carry a small service fee.

Aftercare

Follow the aftercare instructions given to you after your appointment. UPLIFT recommends saline-based aftercare. Avoid rotating jewelry during healing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is UPLIFT's piercing studio in SoHo?

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing is located at 497 Broome St in SoHo, New York City. We are open daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. Walk-ins are welcome for most piercing services.

What jewelry does UPLIFT use for piercings?

UPLIFT uses solid gold and implant-grade titanium for all new piercings. Gold starts at around $99 and includes a lifetime warranty. Titanium starts at around $50. Jewelry is priced separately from the service fee. Outside jewelry is not accepted for new piercings.

Can I bring my own jewelry for a new piercing?

No. All new piercings at UPLIFT require jewelry purchased through the studio. This ensures proper material grade, gauge, and length for healthy healing. Once healed, you may bring your own jewelry for an install appointment.

Does UPLIFT do walk-in piercings?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM for most piercings. For curated ear consultations or specific combinations, booking ahead is recommended to ensure your piercer is available.

What piercings does UPLIFT offer?

UPLIFT offers lobe, helix, flat, tragus, conch, rook, daith, septum, nostril, and curated ear consultations. Contact the studio or book online to confirm availability for specific placements.

Are check-ups free at UPLIFT?

Yes. Check-ups after your piercing are always complimentary. Downsizing appointments and installs may carry a small service fee.
