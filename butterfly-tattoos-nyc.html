What is a fineline butterfly tattoo?

Where butterfly tattoos look best.

Excellent

Inner wrist

Visible, personal, and easy to care for during healing. One of the most popular butterfly placements at UPLIFT.

Excellent

Shoulder blade

FAQ

Butterfly tattoo questions answered.

Where can I get a butterfly tattoo in NYC?

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo at 497 Broome St specializes in fineline and single-needle butterfly tattoos. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. Flash butterfly designs are available same-day. For custom work or booking with Junh, plan ahead.

How much does a butterfly tattoo cost in NYC?

Flash butterfly tattoos at UPLIFT start around $199+. Custom fineline butterfly designs start around $299+. More detailed or larger butterfly pieces are typically $500 or more. Final pricing is confirmed after the artist reviews the design, size, and placement.

Do butterfly tattoos age well?

Fineline butterfly tattoos age best when placed on skin that doesn't stretch or wrinkle significantly - wrists, ankles, shoulders, and upper arms are ideal. Sun exposure is the biggest factor in premature fading. With proper aftercare and sunscreen, a well-executed fineline butterfly can last for many years with minimal loss of detail.

Does Junh at UPLIFT specialize in butterfly tattoos?

Yes. Junh is one of UPLIFT's most requested fineline artists and is particularly known for delicate butterfly and botanical work. Booking with Junh in advance is recommended as their availability fills quickly.

What is the best placement for a butterfly tattoo?

The inner wrist, shoulder blade, ankle, and collarbone are the most popular butterfly tattoo placements at UPLIFT. Each placement suits different sizes and detail levels. Your artist will help you select the right placement for your specific design during the consultation.
