Placement Guide

Choosing placement for matching tattoos.

Matching tattoos look best when both placements mirror each other - same location on both people, or intentionally different placements that complement rather than compete.

  • Inner wrist - visible, easy to compare side by side
  • Ankle or outer calf - symmetrical on both clients
  • Behind the ear - delicate and subtle
  • Upper arm - versatile and easy to conceal
  • Collarbone or sternum - elegant fineline placement

Your artists will help coordinate placement decisions during the consultation so both pieces look intentional together.

FAQ

Matching tattoo questions answered.

Where can I get matching tattoos in NYC?

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo at 497 Broome St offers matching tattoo appointments for couples, friends, and small groups. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. For groups of 3 or more, booking in advance is strongly recommended.

Do matching tattoos have to be identical?

No. Matching tattoos can be identical, mirror images, complementary designs, or variations of the same theme. Split illustrations, same element on different placements, and shared motifs with individual touches are all popular options. Our artists help you find the right balance.

Can two people get tattooed at the same time?

Yes. When multiple artists are available, both clients can be tattooed simultaneously. Booking in advance gives us the best chance to arrange this. Walk-in matching appointments are accepted when availability allows.

How much do matching tattoos cost in NYC?

Matching flash tattoos start around $199+ per person. Custom matching designs start around $299+ per person. Groups of 3+ require a $225 deposit. Final pricing per person depends on size, detail, and placement.

What are the most popular matching tattoo ideas?

The most popular matching tattoos at UPLIFT include sun and moon pairs, matching botanicals and florals, initials and single letters, split illustrations, matching butterflies, and coordinating celestial symbols. We can also help you develop a completely custom matching concept.
