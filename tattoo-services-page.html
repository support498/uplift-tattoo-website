Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM at 497 Broome St in SoHo. Flash designs are available same-day. Custom work may require a booking depending on artist availability.

How much does a tattoo cost at UPLIFT?

Flash tattoos start at $199. Custom tattoos start at $299, and most detailed fineline pieces run $500 or more depending on size and complexity. Final pricing is confirmed after your artist reviews the stencil on the day of your appointment.

What is a fineline tattoo?

A fineline tattoo uses thin, precise lines and delicate shading to create refined, minimal designs. At UPLIFT, fineline work is done by specialists who understand how to create clean lines that hold their shape over time.

How long do fineline tattoos last?

With proper aftercare and sun protection, fineline tattoos can look sharp for many years. Placement matters - areas with less friction and less sun exposure hold detail longer. Our artists ink with longevity in mind.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely. Bring references, sketches, or inspiration images. Your artist will develop a stencil based on your idea, sized and styled for your placement. Final stencil review happens on the day of your appointment.

Do you do group tattoos?

Yes. We love groups - bachelorette parties, NYC visitors, couples, families, and friends. Book with a $225 group deposit and we'll coordinate scheduling for everyone. Walk-in groups are also welcome based on availability.

Is UPLIFT a good studio for a first tattoo?

Yes. Many of our clients are getting their first tattoo. Our artists are patient, communicative, and focused on making the experience comfortable. The studio is clean and easy to find in SoHo - and we're with you through the whole process.
