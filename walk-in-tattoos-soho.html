Walk-In Process

What happens when you walk into UPLIFT.

Step 01

Arrive and browse

Come in any time between 11 AM and 7 PM. Browse the flash collection in-studio, ask questions, and take your time selecting a design and placement.

Step 02

Meet your artist

Available artists are matched to walk-in clients. If you have a preference for a specific artist, calling ahead helps - but is not required for flash work.

Step 03

Stencil and confirm

Your artist creates a stencil and places it for approval. You see the size and placement before any needles are used. Adjustments at this stage are easy.

Step 04

Leave with ink

Most walk-in flash pieces are done in 30-90 minutes. You leave with your tattoo wrapped, full aftercare instructions, and access to our team for any follow-up questions.

Walk-In Policies

What to know before you arrive.

Valid ID required

All clients must be 18 or older with a valid government-issued photo ID. No exceptions. Parental consent does not extend to our walk-in policy.

Flash only for walk-ins

Walk-in availability is for flash designs from our collection. Custom tattoos and bookings with specific artists require advance scheduling.

Arrive before 6 PM

We close at 7 PM. Last walk-in slots are typically accepted by 6 PM to allow adequate time for the session. Arriving early gives you the best chance of same-day availability.

Artist availability varies

Walk-in capacity depends on how many artists are available. Weekdays before 3 PM tend to have the most availability. Weekends can be busy - consider booking in advance.

FAQ

Walk-in tattoo questions answered.

Does UPLIFT Tattoo accept walk-ins in SoHo?

Yes. UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing at 497 Broome St in SoHo accepts walk-ins daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. Flash tattoo designs are available same-day based on artist availability. No deposit is required to walk in.

What tattoos can I get as a walk-in?

Walk-in clients at UPLIFT choose from our flash collection - pre-drawn fineline designs available same-day across categories including butterfly, botanical, minimal, script, and celestial. Custom tattoos and bookings with specific artists require advance scheduling.

How much do walk-in tattoos cost?

Walk-in flash tattoos at UPLIFT start around $199+. Final pricing depends on the specific design and placement. Pricing is confirmed when your artist reviews the selected design on arrival.

What time should I arrive for a walk-in tattoo?

UPLIFT is open for walk-ins from 11 AM to 7 PM daily. Last walk-in slots are typically accepted around 6 PM to allow enough time for the session. For the best chance of same-day availability, arrive before 3 PM - especially on weekends.

Do I need to bring anything?

Bring a valid photo ID. Eat a full meal before you arrive and stay hydrated. Wear or bring clothing that allows easy access to the area you want tattooed. Browse our flash collection online in advance if you already have a design in mind - it saves time in the studio.
