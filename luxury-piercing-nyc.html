Why It's Different

What makes UPLIFT's piercing experience elevated.

Solid gold - not gold-plated

Every gold piece at UPLIFT is solid gold. Not gold-filled. Not gold-plated. Solid. Plated jewelry corrodes, discolors, and is not safe for healing piercings. We do not carry it.

Lifetime warranty on gold

All solid gold jewelry purchased through UPLIFT includes a lifetime warranty. If it breaks, fails, or has a manufacturing defect, we replace or repair it. No expiration.

Implant-grade titanium only

Our titanium jewelry meets ASTM F136 implant-grade standards. It is hypoallergenic, lightweight, and the safest option for clients with metal sensitivities.

No outside jewelry for new piercings

Clients cannot bring their own jewelry for new piercings. This is a safety policy. Improperly sized or low-quality jewelry directly causes healing problems.

Complimentary check-ups

Check-ups are always free. If you have a question about healing, irritation, or jewelry fit - walk in or call. The experience does not end when you leave the studio.

Curated, not rushed

UPLIFT piercers take time with every client. Placement, anatomy, jewelry selection, and aftercare are all part of the appointment - not an afterthought.

FAQ

Luxury piercing questions answered.

Where can I get luxury piercing in NYC?

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo at 497 Broome St offers luxury piercing with solid gold and implant-grade titanium jewelry. Curated ear consultations are available by appointment. Walk-ins are welcome for single piercing appointments during studio hours.

What makes a piercing studio luxury?

A luxury piercing studio uses high-quality jewelry (solid gold or implant-grade titanium), employs trained piercers, provides a clean and curated environment, and focuses on the client's long-term result rather than volume. At UPLIFT, jewelry quality and placement precision are the priority.

Is solid gold jewelry worth it for piercings?

Yes. Solid gold is one of the most biocompatible metals available and one of the safest for healing piercings. It does not corrode, tarnish, or release particles that irritate tissue. Gold-plated and gold-filled alternatives do not offer the same properties. All UPLIFT gold pieces are solid gold and include a lifetime warranty.

What is curated ear piercing?

Curated ear piercing is the process of designing a personalized combination of piercing placements and jewelry styles that work together aesthetically. UPLIFT in SoHo offers curated ear consultations to help clients build their look over time - planning placement sequences, jewelry pairings, and healing timelines.
