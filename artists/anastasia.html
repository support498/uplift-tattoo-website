About Anastasia

Where realism meets refinement.

Anastasia brings a high-touch approach to every appointment - from the first consultation through the final stencil review. Her work lives in the intersection of micro-realism and delicate fineline, with pieces that carry photographic-level detail at a fraction of the scale. Clients seeking meaningful, custom pieces that require nuance and care book with Anastasia specifically for her ability to translate complex concepts into refined fineline work.

All appointments at UPLIFT include a full consultation, stencil review, and aftercare guidance. Walk-ins are welcome for flash work based on availability. Booking in advance is recommended to secure a session with Anastasia.
