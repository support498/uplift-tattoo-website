How It Works

Your piercing appointment at UPLIFT.

Step 01

Book or walk in

Piercing appointments are available by booking or walk-in during studio hours. Curated ear consultations benefit from advance scheduling.

Step 02

Select jewelry

Choose from our curated selection of solid gold and titanium pieces. Your piercer helps you find the right style, size, and metal for your placement and anatomy.

Clean, precise, and fast. Your piercer marks the placement for your approval before proceeding. The piercing itself takes seconds.

Full aftercare instructions provided before you leave. Complimentary check-ups available any time. Downsizing appointments available after initial healing.

FAQ

Piercing questions answered.

What piercing services does UPLIFT offer in SoHo?

UPLIFT offers ear piercing (lobe, helix, tragus, conch, daith, rook), nose piercing (nostril and septum), and select body piercing services. Curated ear piercing consultations are available for clients designing a full ear look. All piercings use solid gold or implant-grade titanium jewelry purchased through UPLIFT.

Can I bring my own jewelry for a new piercing?

No. UPLIFT does not accept outside jewelry for new piercings. All jewelry must be purchased through UPLIFT to ensure quality, safety, and proper fit. This policy directly protects your healing.

How much does piercing cost at UPLIFT?

Jewelry pricing is separate from the service fee. Implant-grade titanium jewelry starts around $50 and solid gold starts around $99. Service fees vary by placement. Contact us or visit in person for specific pricing.

Does UPLIFT do curated ear piercing?

Yes. Curated ear piercing - designing a personalized combination of ear placements and jewelry - is one of UPLIFT's most popular services. Book a consultation to plan your ear project, whether you are adding one piece or building a full curated look from scratch.

What does the lifetime warranty on gold jewelry cover?

All solid gold jewelry purchased through UPLIFT comes with a lifetime warranty. If a piece breaks, fails, or has a defect, we will replace or repair it. This applies to all solid gold pieces - not gold-filled or plated items, which we do not carry.
