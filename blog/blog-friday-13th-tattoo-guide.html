Friday the 13th Tattoo Guide NYC

By UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing  |  SoHo, New York City

Friday the 13th tattoo events are the most spontaneous, accessible, and social tattoo experiences available. When the 13th falls on a Friday, UPLIFT in SoHo releases a special-edition flash collection - designs made specifically for the day, available as walk-ins. It is the best day to get your first tattoo, get tattooed with friends, or add something quick and meaningful to your collection.

UPLIFT hosts Friday the 13th flash events at 497 Broome St in SoHo whenever the date falls. Here is everything you need to know to make the most of it - whether this is your first tattoo event or your sixth.

What Is a Friday the 13th Tattoo Event?

The tradition of Friday the 13th flash events dates back decades in the tattoo industry. The concept: a studio releases a collection of special designs available only for the day, typically at accessible pricing, as walk-ins. The energy is different from a regular appointment - studios are full, artists are working back-to-back, and the shared experience of everyone getting tattooed on the same day creates an atmosphere unlike any other.

At UPLIFT, Friday the 13th events feature fineline flash designs created specifically for the occasion - not the standard everyday collection. The designs change with each event.

How It Works at UPLIFT

  • Follow @uplifttattoo on Instagram for event announcements and design reveals before the day
  • Walk in on the day starting at 11 AM - no appointment required for flash event designs
  • Arrive early for the best selection and shorter wait times
  • Browse the event flash sheet, choose a design and placement, and get tattooed
  • Event designs are typically available until artists are fully booked for the day
  • Bring valid photo ID, eat before arriving, and wear clothing that allows access to the placement

Tips for Getting the Most Out of a Flash Event

  • Arrive early - The most popular designs go quickly. Coming in at or near opening gives you the most options.
  • Have a placement in mind - Know where you want the tattoo so there is no uncertainty when you arrive.
  • Be flexible on exact design - Events are high-volume days. If your first-choice design is taken, the flash collection will have something else you love.
  • Bring a friend - Friday the 13th events are ideal for two people getting tattooed together. The energy is already there.
  • Eat before you come - A full meal before a walk-in tattoo is always the right move.
  • Check Instagram beforehand - The flash sheet is usually posted before the event day so you can plan your design and placement in advance.

Is a Flash Event a Good Choice for a First Tattoo?

Yes - one of the best. The low-pressure, same-day, clearly priced format removes almost all of the uncertainty that comes with a first tattoo. You do not need to plan a design in advance, there is no blank page to fill, and the artist is experienced with high-volume first-timer appointments on event days. First-time clients consistently describe flash events as their most natural entry point into tattooing.

Standard flash designs are available year-round for walk-in appointments. Friday the 13th event designs are exclusive to each event and are not available afterward.
