Couples Tattoo Ideas

By UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing  |  SoHo, New York City

Couples tattoos are one of the most requested appointment types at UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo. Partners, newlyweds, long-term couples, and people marking a significant moment book with us every week.

If you are looking for ideas or trying to figure out what works best, here is a full guide.

Classic Couples Tattoo Ideas

Coordinates

Where you met, where you got engaged, or a city that belongs to both of you. Clean fineline lettering, minimal and permanent.

Matching Dates

A significant date in Roman numerals or clean numerical format. Simple, personal, and timeless.

Sun and Moon

Complementary symbols - one person gets the sun, the other the moon. Connected but distinct.

Matching Initials

Each person's initials, or each other's. Subtle, personal, and easy to wear anywhere.

Matching Florals

The same flower on each person, or complementary flowers from a shared story. Beautiful in fineline.

Short Phrase

A word or short phrase that means something specific to both of you. Handwriting-style lettering feels personal.

Matching Small Symbols

Stars, moons, arrows, waves - the same small design in matching or mirror placements.

Matching Butterflies

Identical or mirrored fineline butterflies. One of the most requested designs for couples at UPLIFT.

Complementary Couples Tattoo Ideas

Some of the best couples tattoos are not identical - they are two halves of a whole. These feel more intentional and personal:

  • Lock and key
  • Sun and crescent moon
  • Two birds facing each other
  • Mountain and wave - one each
  • Day and night - one element from each
  • First and last word of a meaningful phrase, split between two people
  • Two halves of a single image that connect when held side by side
  • Yin and yang, each person wearing one half

Placement Ideas for Couples Tattoos

The same placement on both people creates the strongest visual connection. Popular couples placements:

  • Inner wrist - Visible, intimate, easy to show or cover
  • Inner forearm - Readable and accessible for most designs
  • Collarbone - Elegant and symmetrical for matching designs
  • Ankle - Small designs, easy to match
  • Behind the ear - Subtle and personal for micro designs
  • Ribcage / side - More private, for designs you want to keep between the two of you

What to Think About Before Booking

  • Size - Smaller designs age better for couples tattoos and give more placement options.
  • Style - Fineline is the most requested style for couples tattoos because it works across all aesthetics and skin tones.
  • Meaning vs. trend - Choose something personal over something currently popular. Personal designs hold meaning longer.
  • Will you both love it in 20 years? - Classic and simple always wins long-term.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are good couples tattoo ideas?

Coordinates, dates, sun and moon pairs, matching initials, complementary symbols, short phrases, matching florals, and small identical designs are all popular choices. Fineline style works best for most couples tattoos.

Do couples tattoos have to match exactly?

No. Complementary designs - like sun and moon, lock and key, or two halves of a larger image - are often more interesting and personal than identical tattoos.
