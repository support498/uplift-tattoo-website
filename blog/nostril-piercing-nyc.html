Nostril Piercing NYC: Cost, Healing Time, and Jewelry Guide

By UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing  ·  Updated May 2025  ·  SoHo, New York City

Quick Answer

How much does a nostril piercing cost in NYC?

A nostril piercing at UPLIFT in SoHo includes a service fee plus jewelry. Implant-grade titanium jewelry starts around $50 and solid gold starts around $99. Jewelry is priced separately from the service fee. Complimentary check-ups are always included.

What is a nostril piercing?

A nostril piercing is placed through the soft tissue on either side of the nose, typically just above the curve of the nostril. It is one of the most popular facial piercings in NYC and one of the most versatile in terms of jewelry options - from delicate studs to small rings and hoops.

Does a nostril piercing hurt?

Most clients rate nostril piercings at a 4-5 out of 10. The initial pinch is quick, followed by a brief stinging sensation and eyes watering - a reflexive response, not a pain indicator. The actual piercing takes less than a second when performed by an experienced piercer.

Nostril piercing healing time

Nostril piercings typically take 4-6 months to fully heal, though surface healing occurs within 6-8 weeks. Cartilage piercings heal from the outside in, which means they can appear healed before the tissue underneath is fully recovered. Avoid changing jewelry until your piercer confirms full healing.

The nostril is cartilage, which means it is slower to heal than soft tissue piercings like lobes. Patience and consistent aftercare make the difference between a seamless heal and ongoing irritation.

Jewelry options for nostril piercings

At UPLIFT, all new nostril piercings begin with a flat-back labret stud or L-shaped post in implant-grade titanium or solid gold. This style minimizes movement and promotes stable healing. After healing, clients can transition to seamless rings, hoops, or decorative studs.

  • Implant-grade titanium flat-back stud - starting around $50
  • Solid gold L-shaped post - starting around $99
  • Seamless rings and hoops available after healing

Where to get a nostril piercing in NYC

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing at 497 Broome St in SoHo offers professional nostril piercing with solid gold and implant-grade titanium jewelry. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. Complimentary check-ups are always included.

Nostril piercing aftercare

  • Clean twice daily with sterile saline spray - spray directly on the piercing
  • Do not touch, rotate, or remove jewelry during healing
  • Avoid makeup, foundation, and powder near the piercing site
  • Sleep on a clean pillowcase - a travel pillow can help reduce pressure on the piercing
  • Avoid swimming pools and ocean water for the first 8 weeks
