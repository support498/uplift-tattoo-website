Gifting a Tattoo in NYC

By UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing  |  SoHo, New York City

A tattoo appointment is one of the most personal and memorable gifts you can give someone. Unlike most gifts, it does not sit unused or get returned - it becomes part of the person who receives it. For a first tattoo, a birthday, a milestone, or a shared experience between two people, a tattoo gift is something they will carry with them permanently.

UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo offers several ways to give a tattoo as a gift - from a credit toward a future appointment to booking a shared experience together. Here is how to approach it.

Options for Gifting a Tattoo

  • Gift a credit toward an appointment - Contact the studio to arrange a gift credit. The recipient comes in and applies the credit toward any flash or custom tattoo. They choose the design, size, and placement with no constraints from the gifter.
  • Book a shared appointment - Come in together. You pay for the appointment as a gift and the recipient gets their tattoo while you are there with them. A shared experience.
  • Book a matching tattoo appointment - Both of you get tattooed - the same design or complementary pieces. A matching appointment turns the gift into something you share permanently.
  • Arrange a group appointment - For a birthday or milestone, bring a small group. Multiple artists work simultaneously so everyone gets tattooed in the same visit. A group deposit of $225 holds the session.
  • Gift a walk-in flash experience - Walk in together on a day you choose, browse available designs, and get tattooed the same day. Spontaneous and immediate - one of the simplest versions of a tattoo gift.

Tattoo Gifts for Specific Occasions

  • Birthday gift - A flash walk-in or a credit toward a custom piece. If you know what the person has been wanting, you can even research the design direction and bring reference.
  • First tattoo gift - One of the most meaningful versions of this. For someone who has talked about wanting a tattoo but has not made the move yet, covering the cost removes one barrier. First-time clients are well-supported at UPLIFT.
  • Anniversary or relationship milestone - Book matching tattoos together as an anniversary gift. Complementary designs or identical small pieces are both popular options.
  • Group gift - Multiple people contributing toward a larger or more detailed custom piece. The person receives a credit they can put toward the full cost.

What to Consider When Gifting a Tattoo

  • The recipient should always choose their own design - avoid deciding what they get as a surprise. The gift is the experience and the credit, not a specific tattoo.
  • A credit with no expiration is the most flexible version of this gift
  • If gifting a flash walk-in, confirm the recipient is ready to go - arriving hungry or unprepared affects the experience
  • Flash designs start around $199 and are the simplest option for a defined gift amount
  • Custom tattoos are typically $299 and up - a larger credit gives more flexibility

To arrange a tattoo gift or book a shared appointment, contact UPLIFT directly or text the concierge line. Walk-ins are available daily from 11 AM to 7 PM for flash designs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you gift a tattoo?

Yes. A credit toward a flash or custom tattoo can be arranged at UPLIFT. The recipient chooses the design. It is one of the most personal and lasting gift options available.

What is a good tattoo gift for someone?

A credit toward any appointment gives the most freedom. A matching tattoo appointment together is a more experiential option. For a first tattoo, a walk-in flash appointment is the simplest version.

Can I go to a tattoo appointment with someone else?

Yes. Bringing one guest is typically fine. Group appointments for 2 to 6 people can be booked with multiple artists working simultaneously. A $225 group deposit holds the session.
