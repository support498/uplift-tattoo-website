The Short Answer

In NYC, most tattoos start around $199 for flash designs and $299 for custom work. At UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo, meaningful fineline pieces typically range from $500 to $1,000 or more depending on size, detail, and placement. Pricing is always confirmed with your artist before the session begins.

What Determines Tattoo Pricing?

No two tattoos are priced exactly the same. A tattoo artist quotes based on the real time and skill required for your specific piece - not a flat chart. These are the main factors that move the price up or down.

Size

Larger tattoos take more time. Even detailed small pieces can take longer than a clean mid-size design.

Placement

Standard placements like the forearm, wrist, or shoulder are baseline. Fingers, ribs, hands, feet, and neck carry premium pricing.

Design Complexity

Fine detail, micro-realism, shading, or custom layout all add to the session time and skill required.

Artist Experience

Senior artists and specialists typically price higher. Their quality and waitlists reflect demand.

Flash vs. Custom

Flash tattoos are pre-designed and usually priced lower. Custom pieces require design time, consultation, and stencil preparation.

Color

Color tattoos take longer than black work. Most fineline pieces at UPLIFT are black ink only for the cleanest long-term result.

Premium Placement Pricing

Certain body placements carry a higher starting price. This is standard across NYC studios and reflects the technical difficulty involved - not a penalty for the client.

Premium placements at UPLIFT include:

  • Fingers - fine detail, small canvas, frequent contact with water and surfaces
  • Hands - high movement, skin behavior, sun exposure
  • Ribs - curved surface, movement during breathing, more technical to stencil
  • Neck - visibility, precision required
  • Feet - high fade rate, technical application
  • Inner lip / sternum - specialty placements that require extra care

Your artist will confirm pricing at the start of your appointment for any premium placement.

Deposits and Booking Fees

All appointments at UPLIFT require a non-refundable booking deposit. The deposit is applied to your total on the day of your appointment.

  • Flash deposit: $100
  • Custom deposit: $100
  • Group deposit: $225 (covers 3 or more people)
  • Large project / full day deposit: $550

Deposits can be transferred one time with advance notice. They are not refundable if you cancel without notice or do not show.

Tipping Your Artist

Tipping is not required but is always appreciated. Most clients tip between 15 and 20 percent of the tattoo price. For exceptional work on a large or meaningful piece, 20 to 25 percent is common.

You can tip in cash or ask the front desk about adding a tip digitally when you check out.

NYC Tattoo Pricing vs. Other Cities

Tattoo pricing in New York City is generally higher than most other markets. This reflects the city's cost of living, studio rent, and the density of skilled artists who command premium rates.

At UPLIFT, pricing reflects the full experience - not just the tattoo itself. That includes the consultation, the curated studio environment in SoHo, the quality of materials, the artist's expertise, and the aftercare guidance you receive.

If you are comparing quotes across studios, make sure you are comparing similar work - style, size, placement, and artist experience level all affect price significantly.

Walk-In Pricing

Walk-in tattoos are available daily at UPLIFT from 11 AM to 7 PM. Walk-ins are typically flash designs from the available collection for that day. Walk-in pricing follows flash starting rates of $199 and up.

No deposit is required for walk-ins - you pay on the day. If a design is not available as a walk-in flash, the front desk can help you book a custom appointment.
