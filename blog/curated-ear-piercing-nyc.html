Curated Ear Piercing NYC: How to Design Your Ear and Where to Start

By UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing  ·  Updated May 2025  ·  SoHo, New York City

Quick Answer

What is curated ear piercing?

Curated ear piercing is the process of designing a personalized combination of ear piercing placements and jewelry styles that work together as a cohesive aesthetic. At UPLIFT in SoHo, curated ear consultations help clients plan their ear project from the first new piercing to the finished look - including placement sequence, jewelry selection, and healing timelines.

What makes an ear curated?

A curated ear is intentional. Each piercing placement and jewelry selection is chosen to work with the others - in terms of spacing, jewelry style, metal type, and overall aesthetic. The result is an ear that looks designed rather than accumulated.

Curated ear piercing has become one of the most requested services at NYC piercing studios, particularly among clients who already have one or two piercings and want to build toward a more considered look.

Popular placements for a curated ear in NYC

  • Lobe (first, second, or third) - the foundation of most curated ears
  • Helix - outer upper cartilage, the most common cartilage addition
  • Flat - flat area of inner upper cartilage, great for statement pieces
  • Tragus - small bump in front of the ear canal
  • Conch - inner or outer conch for bold statement pieces
  • Rook - inner cartilage fold above the tragus
  • Daith - innermost cartilage fold near the ear canal

How to plan your curated ear

Most clients do not plan to build a curated ear from the beginning. It starts with one piercing and grows from there. A consultation at UPLIFT helps you assess what you currently have, identify which placements would work with your anatomy, and sequence new piercings so they heal without interfering with each other.

Not all placements can be done simultaneously. Adjacent cartilage piercings need space from each other during healing. Your piercer will help you sequence additions in a way that maximizes both healing success and the final aesthetic result.

Jewelry for a curated ear

The jewelry makes the curation. At UPLIFT, we carry solid gold and implant-grade titanium pieces designed to work together - matching metal types, complementary styles, and cohesive scale. A mix of studs, hoops, huggies, and statement pieces across different placements creates the layered, collected look that defines a curated ear.

  • Solid gold jewelry starts around $99 - lifetime warranty included
  • Implant-grade titanium starts around $50 - hypoallergenic and lightweight
  • Mix and match metals for a modern, mixed-metal curated look
