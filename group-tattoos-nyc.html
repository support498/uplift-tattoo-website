How It Works

Booking a group tattoo appointment at UPLIFT.

Step 01

Contact or book online

For groups of 3 or more, reach out ahead of time. We confirm artist availability and time slots based on group size.

Step 02

Secure with deposit

A $225 group deposit holds the booking and confirms multiple artist slots. The deposit is applied to your total on the day.

Step 03

Choose your designs

Each person can pick from our flash collection or request a custom design. Groups using the same design speed up the process significantly.

Step 04

Get tattooed together

Multiple artists work simultaneously when possible. The group moves through the studio at a steady pace. Everyone leaves with aftercare guidance.

FAQ

Group tattoo questions answered.

Can a group get tattoos together in NYC?

Yes. UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo accommodates groups of all sizes with advance booking. A $225 group deposit secures multiple artist slots. Groups of 5 or more should contact us directly to plan timing and artist availability.

Does UPLIFT do bachelorette tattoo parties?

Yes. UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo regularly hosts bachelorette and bridal party tattoo appointments. Book in advance to secure multiple artist slots and ensure a smooth flow for the whole group. Flash designs are popular for these events because every participant can choose their own piece.

Do all members of the group have to get the same tattoo?

No. Each person in the group can choose their own design from our flash collection, request a custom piece, or opt for a shared theme. Many groups pick from the same flash sheet but select different individual designs from within it.

How far in advance should a group book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks ahead for groups of 4 or more. Same-week availability exists for smaller groups depending on artist schedules. Contact us directly for large groups or event-based activations - those require more lead time.

Does UPLIFT offer tattoo activations for events?

Yes. UPLIFT offers full-service tattoo activations for corporate events, brand launches, fashion shows, and private parties. Licensed and insured artists, custom flash design, full setup, and guest management are all included. Pricing starts around $2,000 per artist per hour. Contact us for a custom proposal.
