How Flash Works

Walk in. Pick it. Done.

01

Browse & Pick

Walk in and see what is on the flash wall. Follow @uplifttattoo on Instagram for a preview of current designs. No appointment needed for flash.

Confirm Size & Placement

Tell your artist where you want it and what size works. They stencil it on your skin so you can see exactly how it looks before anything starts.

03

Most flash pieces take 30 minutes to an hour depending on size and detail. Same-day, no long wait, no months-long booking process.

Full written instructions provided. Heal it correctly and it will look clean for years. We are reachable after your appointment for any healing questions.

Questions

Flash FAQ

Walk In or Book

What is a flash tattoo?

A flash tattoo is a pre-designed piece that is ready to tattoo the same day. Browse available designs, pick one, and get it done during your visit - no custom design process required.

Can I walk in for a flash tattoo?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM at 497 Broome St in SoHo. Flash designs are available same-day with no appointment needed.

How much does a flash tattoo cost?

Flash tattoos at UPLIFT start at $199. Final price is based on size, placement, and detail level. Your artist confirms the price before anything starts.

Can I modify a flash design?

Minor adjustments like sizing and placement are standard. Significant design changes may convert it to a custom appointment. Ask your artist - they will advise what is possible.

How do I know what flash is available?

Walk in to see the full wall. You can also follow @uplifttattoo on Instagram where we post available designs regularly, or text the concierge to ask about current availability.

Are flash tattoos permanent?

Yes. Flash tattoos are fully permanent. The only difference from custom work is that the design is pre-made. The tattooing process, healing, and aftercare are identical.
