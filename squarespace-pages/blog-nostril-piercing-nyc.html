Nostril Piercing in NYC - What to Know

A complete guide to nostril piercings at UPLIFT in SoHo - placement, jewelry, and healing timeline.

What Is a Nostril Piercing?

A nostril piercing is one of the most popular facial piercings in the world. Placement is typically on one side of the nose, just above the curve of the nostril. Bilateral (paired) nostril piercings are also a popular choice. The piercing is quick and recovery is manageable with proper care.

Healing Time

Nostril piercings typically take 4-6 months to heal externally and up to 12 months for full internal healing. During this time, your jewelry should not be changed by anyone other than a professional.

Nostril Jewelry Styles

  • Flat-back labret studs - most comfortable for healing
  • Small studs with gemstones or plain gold balls
  • Seamless hoops after full healing
  • 14k and 18k solid gold for long-term wear

Jewelry for Your Nostril Piercing

At UPLIFT, all new piercing jewelry is solid gold or implant-grade titanium. Gold jewelry starts at $99+. Titanium options start at $50+. All gold pieces include a lifetime warranty.

Aftercare for Your Nostril Piercing

  • Clean twice daily with sterile saline spray
  • Do not touch the piercing with unwashed hands
  • Avoid rotating or moving the jewelry during healing
  • Schedule a downsize appointment after initial healing begins
