Everything about helix piercings at UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo - placement, jewelry, and healing.

What Is a Helix Piercing?

A helix piercing is placed on the upper outer rim of the ear cartilage. It is one of the most versatile ear piercings - it can be worn as a single stud, stacked with other helix piercings, or integrated into a curated ear. Healing takes longer than lobe piercings due to the cartilage, but the result is worth it.

Healing Time

Helix piercings typically take 9-12 months to fully heal. Cartilage is slower to heal than soft tissue. Avoid sleeping on the pierced ear during initial healing to reduce irritation.

Helix Jewelry Styles

  • Flat-back labret studs - best for initial healing
  • Small hoops and clickers after healing
  • Stacking multiple helix piercings along the rim
  • Solid gold for a refined, elevated look

Aftercare for Your Helix Piercing

  • Clean twice daily with sterile saline spray
  • Do not touch the piercing with unwashed hands
  • Avoid rotating or moving the jewelry during healing
  • Schedule a downsize appointment after initial healing begins
