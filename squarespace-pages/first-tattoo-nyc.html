What Should I Know Before My First Tattoo?

Your first tattoo doesn't have to be intimidating. At UPLIFT, our artists specialize in clean, delicate fineline work - the kind that looks refined and heals beautifully. Most first tattoos are small, meaningful pieces that take 30 to 90 minutes.

The most important things to do before your appointment: eat a meal beforehand, stay hydrated, wear comfortable clothing that allows access to your chosen placement, and bring clear reference images or a few ideas for your artist to work from.

First Tattoo FAQ

Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Pain varies by placement. Fleshy areas like the outer arm, thigh, or calf tend to be the least sensitive. Areas close to bone or with thin skin - like ribs, spine, or fingers - are more intense. Most clients describe fineline work as a light scratching sensation.

How much does a first tattoo cost?

Flash tattoos start at $199+. Custom pieces start at $299+. Lettering starts at $299+. Final pricing depends on size, placement, and detail. A $100 deposit is required at booking and applied to your total.

How do I prepare for my first tattoo?

Eat a full meal 1-2 hours before. Stay hydrated. Get good sleep the night before. Wear loose clothing that gives easy access to your chosen placement. Avoid alcohol 24 hours before.

What style is best for a first tattoo?

Fineline and minimalist tattoos are excellent choices for first-timers. They're clean, heal well, and tend to be faster sessions. Flash designs are also great - they're pre-approved for quality and come with set pricing.

Can I walk in for my first tattoo?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM. Flash designs are the most reliable walk-in option. For a custom first tattoo, booking ahead ensures you get the right artist and time for your vision.
