Walk-In Tattoo FAQ

Do I need an appointment for a walk-in tattoo?

No. Walk-ins are welcome daily from 11 AM to 7 PM at 497 Broome St, SoHo. Slots are first-come, first-served based on artist availability that day.

What kinds of tattoos are available as walk-ins?

Flash designs are the best option - they're ready-made, priced, and can be done same day. Custom walk-ins may be possible depending on complexity and the day's schedule.

How much do walk-in tattoos cost?

Flash tattoos start at $199+. Pricing depends on the specific design. You'll see set prices with each design in our flash collection.

Is there a deposit for walk-ins?

Walk-in flash tattoos typically don't require a pre-paid deposit. You pay at time of service. Booked appointments require a $100 deposit.

What should I bring for a walk-in?

Bring a valid ID and arrive hydrated. For arm, leg, or ankle placement - wear loose clothing for easy access. Cash and cards both accepted.
