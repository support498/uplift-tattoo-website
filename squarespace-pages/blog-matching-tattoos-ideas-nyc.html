Matching Tattoo Ideas for NYC

Coordinated designs for friends, couples, siblings, and families. Ideas from UPLIFT's fineline studio in SoHo.

Why Get Matching Tattoos?

Matching tattoos are one of the most personal forms of shared expression. They don't need to be identical - they need to be intentional. A shared motif, a split image, or a complementary pair all communicate the same thing: a relationship worth marking.

Matching Tattoo Styles That Work Well

Fineline designs are particularly well-suited for matching tattoos. Clean lines reproduce consistently across different skin tones and placements. Botanical designs, celestial symbols, abstract line art, and small portraits are all strong choices.

For a more personal approach, consider designs that are "broken" between two people - a design that only makes complete sense when both people are together. Or two versions of the same subject interpreted in slightly different ways.

Popular Placement Pairings

  • Inner wrist - visible and easy to compare side by side
  • Forearm - excellent for paired designs with more detail
  • Behind the ear - discreet and intimate
  • Ankle - popular for matching floral or minimal designs
  • Shoulder blade - for larger paired compositions
