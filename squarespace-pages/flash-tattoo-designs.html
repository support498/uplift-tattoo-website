What Is a Flash Tattoo?

A flash tattoo is a pre-designed tattoo that's ready to be applied as-is. At UPLIFT, flash designs are created by our artists and curated for quality - clean fineline work, minimal compositions, and designs that heal well and age gracefully.

Flash tattoos are ideal for walk-ins, same-day decisions, and anyone who wants a great tattoo without a long design consultation. They're priced per design, starting at $199+.

Flash Tattoo FAQ

Can I change a flash design?

Minor size adjustments are usually possible. Significant changes to the design itself would move it into custom territory, with custom pricing. Talk to your artist about what's feasible.

Are flash tattoos lower quality than custom?

Not at UPLIFT. Our flash collection is designed by our own artists and held to the same standard as custom work. Flash just means the design is pre-made - the execution is the same level of craft.

How do I know what flash designs are available?

View available designs on our flash page, walk in to see the current collection, or message us before your visit. Designs rotate as they get claimed.

Can I book a specific flash design in advance?

Yes. Book an appointment and note which flash design or style you're interested in. A $100 deposit holds your slot and is applied to your total.

How much does a flash tattoo cost?

Flash tattoos start at $199+. Pricing is set per design and visible with each piece in the collection. Promotional flash events may have different pricing announced in advance.
