Septum Piercing in NYC - What to Know

Everything about septum piercings at UPLIFT Tattoo & Piercing in SoHo - placement, healing, jewelry, and aftercare.

What Is a Septum Piercing?

A septum piercing goes through the soft tissue at the center of the nose - the "sweet spot" between the cartilage and the tip of the nose. When placed correctly, it sits level and centered, and can be flipped up and hidden inside the nose when needed. Most clients describe the sensation as a quick eye-watering pinch - usually over in seconds.

Healing Time

Septum piercings typically heal in 6-8 months with proper care. Initial jewelry is usually a larger gauge to accommodate swelling. A downsize appointment is recommended after 6-8 weeks.

Jewelry Styles for Septum

  • Seamless clicker rings - the most popular option
  • Horseshoe barbells - easy to flip up
  • Circular barbells - classic and understated
  • Plain gold or titanium for healing; gems and charms after

Jewelry for Your Septum Piercing

At UPLIFT, all new piercing jewelry is solid gold or implant-grade titanium. Gold jewelry starts at $99+. Titanium options start at $50+. All gold pieces include a lifetime warranty.

Aftercare for Your Septum Piercing

  • Clean twice daily with sterile saline spray
  • Do not touch the piercing with unwashed hands
  • Avoid rotating or moving the jewelry during healing
  • Schedule a downsize appointment after initial healing begins
