What Affects Tattoo Pricing?

  • Size - larger tattoos take more time and cost more
  • Detail - intricate fineline work requires more precision and time
  • Placement - premium placements (finger, hand, ribs, neck) start higher
  • Color - color work adds time compared to black and grey
  • Artist experience - senior artists command higher rates
  • Custom design prep - original designs require artist time beyond the session

Are NYC Tattoos More Expensive?

NYC tattoo prices reflect the cost of operating a professional studio in Manhattan. That means higher-quality materials, experienced artists, and a studio environment that meets professional safety standards. At UPLIFT, pricing reflects the quality and care behind the work - not an arbitrary premium.
