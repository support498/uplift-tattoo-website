Fineline Tattoo FAQ

What makes a tattoo "fineline"?+

Fineline tattoos are created using very thin needles - often a single needle - to produce delicate, precise lines. The result is a light, minimal aesthetic compared to traditional or bold tattoo styles. UPLIFT specializes in this technique.

Do fineline tattoos fade faster?+

Fineline tattoos can show fading faster in certain placements if the technique or aftercare isn't correct. At UPLIFT, artists use proper depth and placement to maximize longevity. Sun protection and moisturizing extend results significantly.
