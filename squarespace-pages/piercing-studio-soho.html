Piercing Studio FAQ

Can I bring my own jewelry for a new piercing?

No. For new piercings, all jewelry must be purchased through UPLIFT. This ensures the material, sizing, and gauge are appropriate for initial healing. Outside jewelry is not accepted for fresh piercings.

How much does jewelry cost?

Gold jewelry starts at $99+. Titanium jewelry starts at $50+. Jewelry is priced separately from the piercing service fee. Your piercer will walk you through options during your appointment.

What is the lifetime warranty on gold?

All solid gold jewelry purchased at UPLIFT includes a lifetime warranty on the piece itself. If the clasp breaks, the stone falls out, or there's a material defect, bring it back and we'll make it right.

How long does healing take?

Lobe piercings typically heal in 6-9 months. Cartilage piercings take 9-18 months for full healing. Your piercer will advise on aftercare and when to schedule a downsize appointment.

Do you offer walk-in piercings?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome daily 11 AM - 7 PM, subject to piercer availability. For curated ear appointments or multiple piercings, booking ahead is recommended.
